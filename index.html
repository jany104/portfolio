Power Sense

Developed for the Hult Prize Nationals, Power Sense is a smart energy monitoring system designed to track real-time power usage and cost for households, hostels, and small businesses. The goal is to promote energy awareness and help users reduce electricity consumption through live data insights. The system includes a physical meter and a web dashboard, offering an affordable and user-friendly solution for everyday energy management.
